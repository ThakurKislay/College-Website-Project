Admission courses

Procedure for AKTU Courses

• Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) conducts an entrance examination for 85% seats.The candidates are allotted branches on the basis of their merit through counseling conducted by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU).

• For 15% seats candidates can apply for direct admission to the MAKEA. Admission will be based on the percentage of marks obtained in qualifying exam and /or respective entrance exam as per the policies of the University.

• Candidates seeking admission to the first semester of the six-semester MCA course are required to qualify in the Common Entrance Examination (UPSEE) for admission to Master of Computer Application.

• The eligibility criteria for direct admission on seats remaining vacant in first year after entrance examination counseling shall be such as may be notified from time to time by AKTU.

Hostal View

Though away from home yet college boarding facilities make it a sweet home. Boarding facilities at college for students of residential programs consist of 4 hostels available for boys and girls separately. The institutes have self-contained hostels with more than 400 rooms in total having the capacity of 800 students on twin-sharing basis. Other facilities include excellent dining halls, indoor games, exercising equipments, communication center with internet facility and sports fields for outdoor games.

• Rooms are spacious, hygienic, well-ventilated and fully furnished for its residents.

• Nutritious meals are served in hostel dining halls in hygienic environment.

• green lawns in the campus always freshen up the hostellers besides being main attraction for those who love nature.

• Twenty Four hours water and electricity supply is available.
